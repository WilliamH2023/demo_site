About Us

Founded in 2020, W Ballroom is a ballroom dance studio based in Medford, MA. W Ballroom
offers instruction in the two styles of competitive partnered ballroom dance: International
Standard and International Latin. Through weekly group lessons, open practices, and private
instruction, members routinely learn new figures, solidify partner work, and fine tune
fundamental dancing techniques.
